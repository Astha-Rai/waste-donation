🌱 About Kindway

Creating a cleaner, greener and kinder world through thoughtful donations.

🌍 Our Vision

To create a sustainable India by bridging citizens with NGOs that need their support — reducing waste, nurturing nature, and building a responsible donation culture.

🌿 Our Mission

We empower people to donate reusable resources, support communities, and minimize environmental harm through tech-enabled pickups, eco-routing, and collaboration with verified NGOs.
